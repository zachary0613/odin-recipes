Carne Asada

Buy some cheap skirt steak, blend up a nice marinade, chef it up and serve it to your favorite people. If you combine this recipe with the Salsa Verde you can't lose. If you're overwhelmed by the ingredients list, dont be afraid to replace or substitute. The marinade does not need to be perfect. You just need some citrus and veggies to give it a flavor boost.

Ingredients

The Marinade

  • 1 Skirt Steak (Hangar steak too)
  • 2 Jalapenos
  • 1 Yellow Onion and 1 Tomato
  • One Can of Beer
  • 1/3 cup of orange juice
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • Chili Powder
  • Cilantro

The Optional Ingredients

  • Tortillas (corn or flour)
  • Limes (for garnish)
  • Avocado (if you want)
  • Beans of some sort

Creation

  1. Broil your tomato, jalapeno, and onion in the oven until cooked and slightly charred..
  2. Combine all the ingredients for the marinade in a blender or food processor and pur√©e.(please be careful with the beer)
  3. Rub the marinade on the steak and store for at least overnight
  4. Cook quickly on a grill or on a cast iron pan and sear till medium rare.
