Butter Chicken over Jasmine Rice

Incredibly cheap comfort food that warms the soul

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds of chicken thighs
  • 1 cup of Greek Yogurt
  • 1 Yellow Onion
  • Knob of ginger
  • Paprika, Cumin, Garam masala, and Turmeric
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • 1 cup of Heavy Cream
  • 14 oz Crushed Tomatoes

Creation

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together yogurt, garam masala, and salt. Toss chicken and Marinate
  2. Sear chicken in a large pot then remove and add oil, garlic, ginger and onion minced
  3. Sautee until softened then add spices and toast for 2 minutes
  4. After toasting, add in crushed tomatoes and 2/3 cup of water then stir until incorporated over medium heat. Let simmer for 5-8 minutes until the sauce has reduced. .
  5. Once reduced add chicken back in and stir in heavy cream.
  6. Removed from heat and stir in butter
  7. Prepare white rice and garnish with cilantro
