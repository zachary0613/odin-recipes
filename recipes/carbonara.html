Carbonara

A cheap, but classy date night meal that cooks in 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 Bacon, Pancetta, or Guanciale
  • 1 Clove of Garlic
  • Bucatini Pasta from Eataly (the best)
  • 3 Large eggs and 1 yolk
  • 4 Ounces of Parmesan
  • Butter

Creation

  1. Dice the meat of choice into small cubes and cook on low to render the fat out
  2. In a large bowl or measuring cup, combine 3 large eggs and 1 egg yolk.
  3. Combine grated cheese and eggs and beat together with a fork until homogenous.
  4. Season with black pepper generously
  5. Cook your noodles in a large salted pot of water
  6. Combine cooked pasta with cooked bacon. Stir in egg mixture and cup of pasta water and mix until creamy
  7. Add parmesan and black pepper to taste
