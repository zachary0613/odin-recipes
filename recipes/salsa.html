Salsa Verde

A great salsa recipe that takes 20 minutes max to chef up and goes great on carne asada

Ingredients

  • 6 Tomatillos
  • 1 Jalapeno
  • 1 White Onion
  • 1/2 of an Avocado
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • Cilantro

Creation

  1. Char the tomatillos, jalapeno and onion in the oven till dark
  2. Blend the charred ingredients until chunky then add the garlic and cilantro
  3. Add avocado till smooth and homogenous
  4. Enjoy
