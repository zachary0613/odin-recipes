Reverse Seared Octopus

The tenderest Octopus you'll ever have

Ingredients

  • 1 Octopus
  • 1 wine cork
  • Olive Oil
  • Calabrian Chiles
  • Some cloves of garlic

Creation

  1. Combine the octopus with some garlic and chiles and coat in olive oil and place in a large baking dish
  2. Cover and cook in the oven for 2 hours at 300 degrees
  3. Remove from the oven and sear on the pan till crispy
